🛡️ VeriGuard

An autonomous, Open-Source Intelligence (OSINT) fact-checking engine that mathematically evaluates claims to eliminate generative AI hallucinations in real-time.

📥 Download Chrome Extension

Installation Instructions

  1. Click the button above to download veriguard.zip.
  2. Extract (unzip) the folder anywhere on your computer.
  3. Open Google Chrome and navigate to chrome://extensions/ in your address bar.
  4. Toggle on Developer mode in the top right corner.
  5. Click the Load unpacked button in the top left.
  6. Select the extracted veriguard_extensions folder.
  7. Pin VeriGuard to your toolbar, highlight any text on the web, and right-click to verify!
